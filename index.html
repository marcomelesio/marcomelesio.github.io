The Overtone Series

The Overtone Series is the basis of tonal music, it describes
the series of pitches of waveforms that are played when a given
sound is recorded. Most modern music is based on this, and through
synthesis, it gives us an idea of how to create different sounds
out of pure waves by simly following the overtone series.

  
  The Overtone Series follows a pattern of frequencies for proper tonal harmony.
  Of which can be changed or followed through synthesis. The Overtone Series
  multiplies the fundamental frequency with every overtone that is added.
